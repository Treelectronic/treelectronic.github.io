# The Stoic Journey — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 5, 2026_

The Stoic Journey ("the app") is built by Treelectronic. We collect as little as possible, and everything meaningful stays under your control.

## What stays on your device
Your journal entries, reading progress, lesson completions, streaks, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device. We cannot read them.

## Optional account and sync
If you create an account (email and password), your journal entries, progress, and favorites are synced to our backend (hosted on Supabase) so you can restore them on another device. Sync is optional — the app is fully functional without an account. You can delete your account and synced data by contacting us at the address below.

## Analytics (optional)
The app records a small set of anonymous usage events (for example: "a lesson was completed", "the daily meditation was viewed") via PostHog to help us understand what is useful. This never includes the text of your journal entries or any content you write. You can turn analytics off at any time in the You tab under Privacy. We do not use advertising identifiers, we show no ads, and we never sell data.

## Crash reports
If the app crashes, a technical crash report (stack trace, device model, OS version) is sent via Sentry so we can fix the bug. Crash reports contain no journal content and no personal identifiers beyond what the crash mechanics require.

## Children
The app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new date.
