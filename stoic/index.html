The Stoic Journey

Two thousand years of practice for a steadier mind.

What it is

  • A meditation each day

    One short passage to sit with, chosen for the day. Enough to steady the morning, small enough to keep.

  • Guided paths

    Ordered lessons that walk you through the core Stoic ideas, one step at a time, at whatever pace suits you.

  • The complete source texts

    Read the works themselves: Marcus Aurelius' Meditations, Seneca's Moral Letters, and Epictetus' Discourses and Enchiridion. Free, no ads, and fully readable offline.

  • An evening journal

    A quiet place to reflect at the end of the day. Your entries stay on your device unless you choose to sync them.
